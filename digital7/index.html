Hamster Pipes

  • High Concept: Help the poor hamster sort out the pipes to go through.
  • Theme: Simple puzzle to help you relax
  • Mandated Variety: Follows very similarly to the puzzle games already like this
  • Player Experience Goals: Preferably the player will be relaxed clicking on the pipes to make them match up
  • Gameplay: The game play is simply click on each tile to rotate them until the line up from start to finish.
  • Prototype Goal: The game mechanic that is available in the prototype is the major gameplay loop that the player goes through. This is used to show the concept of rotating the pipes.
  • Features: The game features colorful pipes with a strong aesthetic. (except for the surrounding art)
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is a backyard as seen by the green representing grass.
  • Story: There will be no major story, a full version of the game would have increasingly difficult puzzles.
  • Target Audience: The demographic that the game is trying to reach is anyone who downloads free ios or android games.
  • Play Time: Very short. Play sessions should only last about a minute max. The full version would similarly take a short amount of time.
  • Strategies: clicking randomly is an effective strategy
  • Prototype Assets: All assets were created by me.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Very closely resembles ios and android puzzle games, where they simple tap to rotate the pipes
